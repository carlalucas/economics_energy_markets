# economics_energy_markets

Group work for the **Economics of Energy Markets** class based on **TREMI 2020** microdata.

## Project topic: energy efficiency policies

Main research questions:
- What fraction of retrofits were supported by public policies ?
- Which loan or financing facilities were used most by households ?
- How prevalent were interactions and cumulative use across policy instruments ?

## Data

Official data source:  
https://www.statistiques.developpement-durable.gouv.fr/la-renovation-energetique-des-logements-bilan-des-travaux-et-des-aides-entre-2016-et-2019-resultats

The project uses the **pseudonymized individual data**.

## Contents

- `01_descriptive_analysis.ipynb`  
  Main notebook.

- `raw/`  
  Raw data and documentation files.

- `figures/`  
  Output graphs and summary tables.

## Main results so far

- Around **50%** of households report at least one public aid.
- Among aided households, nearly **half use two or more aids**.
- The most frequently used public aids are **reduced VAT** and **CITE**.
- Renovation financing is dominated by **self-financing**.
- **Éco-PTZ** remains relatively marginal in observed use.
- Policy interactions are common, with several recurring bundles of aids.
